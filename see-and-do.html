Carntogher Way


The Carntogher Way is a beautiful mountain trail within 10 mins of Maghera. This walk on the hills above the Glenshane Pass gives access to historic Carntogher Mountain with views across Lough Foyle to Errigal and Muckish in Donegal, Sawel and the high Sperrins, and south-east to The Mournes and Slieve Gullion.
More information, including directions, route description and map can be found here.

Portglenone Sunflower Field


Portglenone's beautiful Sunflower field is within a 15 min drive from Maghera. Two fields of tall sunflowers of approximately 175,00 flowers await visitors with plenty of space, as well as wildflowers around the edge. Portstewart Strand


Portstewart Strand is one of Ireland's most beautiful beaches and is only a 25 min drive from Maghera. Make the most of any season and get down on the beach at Portstewart Strand. From wintery walks to summer sand castle competitions there's something for the family to enjoy in all weathers.
